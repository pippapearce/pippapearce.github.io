Hobbies and Interests

I love travelling and exploring new places so I always try and take as many opportunites to experience as much of the world as I can! Spending time with family and friends is where I find my most enjoyment, whether that's curled up on the sofa watching a film, baking anything that comes to mind or going on trips or trying things I have never tried before.

In April 2017 I ran the Southampton Half Marathon, where I raised £784 for Alzheimer's Research UK. This was possibly one of the biggest challenges I have ever faced when training but once the day I arrived I had the time of my life and enjoyed every moment of it, knowing I was running for such an amazing cause.

My main focus at the moment is to build as much experience in the various skills related to digital marketing as possible before I jump into industry once I graduate. This has included taking coding classes with Code First: Girls whereby I won Southampton's Course Competition for creating this website you are seeing here! I also work as a Brand Manager for Union Southampton's comedy club 'Laughter Lounge'.
